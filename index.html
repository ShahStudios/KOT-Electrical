Here at Kot Electrical Services

We have a few core values that we instill in our daily operations. These values are Consistency, Integrity, Respect, Loyalty. What that means for you, is we will consistently do the right thing, respect your home and property while performing the task at hand and we are loyal to our brand knowing that if we follow our core values our work will speak for itself. We are constantly learning with continuing education and trade shows. This allows us to build upon our existing knowledge and experience, to help design lighting with all of the newest products available or when we are changing the main service and panel it is with the safest breaker protection available. Leaving you, our customer, worry-free that the job was completed in the best way possible.

Chris Kot is a 4th generation electrical contractor, holding a master electrician license. Being exposed to the industry at a young age (from the time he could hold a flashlight for his father) he has gained experience from large commercial jobs, older homes rewired and new construction, which has given the him the experience to see every job from multiple different perspectives. His favorite part of the industry is everyday is a new challenge to be solved. Nothing is more rewarding than a happy customer.

Don Pickard has been in the electrical industry for 18+ years, with extensive experience in the residential field. He takes the time with the customers to discuss the project and what to expect before he starts, looking at every possibility to make it happen (he loves to make the impossible happen). The most rewarding part of the industry for him, is the excitement on a customer’s face when they see the finished project.
